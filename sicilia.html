Sicilia

Sicily is sometimes referred to as "God's Kitchen" due to the variety of fresh food used such as seafood, vegetables and grains.

Sicilian cuisine has influences from Italian, Arabic, French and Spanish cuisine.

Pasta con le Sarde

This is a simple but delicious dish, ingredients traditionally include:

  • Pinenuts
  • Fennel
  • Saffron
