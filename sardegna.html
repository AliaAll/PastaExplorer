Sardegna

Sardinian cuisine is as rich and varied as the island itself.

The food reflects the lush Mediterranean Sea, the striking mountains, numerous vineyards and olive groves.

Culurgiones

Culurgiones are a type of stuffed pasta which resemble corn ears.

They are traditionally stuffed with:

  • Potatoes
  • Sardinian pecorino cheese
  • Mint
