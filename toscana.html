Toscana

Tuscan food is famous for being simple, yet comforting and rich in flavour.

Pappardelle alla Lepre

Pappardelle pasta is traditionally eaten with game meat. Hare being the most common.

The dish often includes:

  • Pappardelle
  • Hare
  • Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Meat broth
